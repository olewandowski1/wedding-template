# Wedding Website Requirements: Marzena & Wojciech

## 1. Project Context

- **Page type:** Wedding event website
- **Event type:** Wedding (ceremony and reception)
- **Event format:** In-person
- **Wedding date:** 20 czerwca 2028
- **Location:** Gdańsk, Polska
  - **Ceremony:** Bazylika Mariacka (placeholder)
  - **Reception:** Dwór Artusa (placeholder)
- **Target audience:** Invited guests (family and friends)
- **Primary goal:** Communicate wedding details and collect RSVPs.

---

## 2. Website Vibe & Visual Identity

- **Primary vibe:** **Classic & Elegant**
- **Secondary influence:** Modern Minimal (to keep it clean)
- **Adjectives:** Sophisticated, Timeless, Bright, Regal, Warm
- **5-second impression:** A high-end, formal invitation that feels both celebratory and respectful of tradition.

---

## 3. Color System

- **Primary:** Gold (#D4AF37) – for accents, icons, and highlights.
- **Background:** Soft White / Cream (#FAFAFA) – to maintain a clean, elegant feel.
- **Secondary:** Deep Navy or Charcoal (#2C3E50) – for high-contrast typography.
- **Strategy:** Use gold sparingly to signify luxury without being overwhelming. High contrast for readability by older guests.

---

## 4. Typography

- **Headings:** Elegant Serif (e.g., Playfair Display or Bodoni) – to convey the "Classic" tone.
- **Body:** Clean Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter or Montserrat) – for maximum readability of details.
- **Emphasis:** Italicized serifs for names and quotes. Bold weights for dates and locations.

---

## 5. Theme Strategy

- **Preferred theme:** Light (strictly enforced)
- **Theme switching:** Disabled
- **Justification:** Wedding aesthetics are traditionally light-oriented; ensures visual consistency and accessibility for all age groups.

---

## 6. Page Structure & Narrative Flow

1. **Hero Header:** Names, date, and "The Big Day" announcement.
2. **Invitation Text:** Formal welcome and invitation.
3. **The Couple (Our Story):** A short, elegant "How we met" or "Our Journey" section.
4. **The Event Details:** Two cards/sections for Ceremony and Reception with integrated maps.
5. **Practical Info:** Dress code (Black Tie / Formal), gifts info, and city tips.
6. **RSVP Form:** The final destination for guest interaction.
7. **Footer:** Couple's contact or a simple "We can't wait to see you!"
